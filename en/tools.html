Crisis & Risk Diagnostic

LATAM · May 2026

Three forces are reshaping the operating environment for organizations in LATAM right now: an ongoing conflict in the Strait of Hormuz, Mexico's economic slowdown, and growing US pressure on Mexico over drug trafficking and security. Most executives read these as political or economic context. This tool helps you understand your real exposure — and what communications actions to take before someone else defines the narrative for you.
