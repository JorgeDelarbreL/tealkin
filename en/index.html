About

The experience and thinking behind this
Based in Tulum, Riviera Maya
Available across LATAM
Most organizations invest in communications
without knowing what makes them genuinely different.

Generative AI amplifies the problem: by automating content, it homogenizes design, language, and ideas across entire industries. The result is significant investment with little recall, little differentiation, and minimal impact on the decisions that matter.

TealKin exists to think through that — with tools, analysis, and a perspective built on 14 years at the intersection of corporate communications, crisis management, and stakeholder strategy across Latin America.
